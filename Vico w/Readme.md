Board Overview: ESP32-S3FN8 Dev Board

MCU is an ESP32-S3FN8, dual-core at 240MHz with 8MB flash built in, WiFi and Bluetooth 5.0 LE built in. Power comes from USB-C 5V through an AMS1117-3.3 LDO regulator down to a 3.3V rail. USB is native, no bridge chip needed. Two buttons, one for boot and one for reset. Green power LED on the 3.3V rail. 40MHz crystal. ESD protection on the USB data lines. No antenna on board.

Total ICs is just 1, everything is integrated in the ESP32-S3. BOM cost is around $3.67 at 1k quantity. Target retail price is $7 or under.

Full parts list is 13 caps, 6 resistors, 1 MCU, 1 LDO, 1 crystal, 1 LED, 1 ESD diode, 2 buttons, 1 USB-C connector, and 1 pin header. That is the whole board.
